Model 619/22.01 has a material waste rate of 45.8% — incoming material and process parameters require immediate review.
⚡ 高优先级 — 料废异常⚡ High Priority — Abnormal Material Waste
6202K/01 料废 77.6%（497件），6202K/02 49.2%（231件），集中在 OR/ORSF 工序，生锈是主因（占料废68%）。 Model 6202K/01 has a material waste rate of 77.6% (497 pcs), 6202K/02 at 49.2% (231 pcs), concentrated in OR/ORSF processes. Rust is the leading cause (68% of material waste).
⚠ 关注 — Line 10 料废⚠ Watch — Line 10 Material Waste
Line 10 料废 1,599件，占双线料废总量 91.6%；其中 OR（456件）和 ORSF（629件）工序最突出，系统性问题明显。 Line 10 material waste reaches 1,599 pcs — 91.6% of total material waste across both lines. OR (456 pcs) and ORSF (629 pcs) are the most affected processes, indicating a systemic issue.
⚠ 关注 — 月末废品激增⚠ Watch — Month-End Waste Surge
5月23～25日及30日连续出现高峰（最高428件/天），均以 Bore 工序为主。需核查月末产量冲刺与设备状态。 May 23–25 and May 30 saw consecutive peaks (max 428 pcs/day), primarily in the Bore process. Investigate whether month-end output rushes or equipment degradation are contributing factors.
📌 Bore工序主导📌 Bore Process Dominates
Bore（内径磨）责任废品 2,662件，占后磨总量 53.9%，但料废仅233件（8.7%），说明以加工质量问题为主而非来料问题。 Bore (inner diameter grinding) accounts for 2,662 pcs of responsibility waste — 53.9% of the back-grinding total — yet material waste is only 233 pcs (8.7%), pointing to process quality rather than incoming material issues.
📌 ORSF料废双高📌 ORSF — Dual High Waste
ORSF（外沟超精）责任废901件+料废670件，料废占比 42.6%；其中生锈516件、污渍124件，提示防锈与储存管理问题。 ORSF (outer ring superfinish) has 901 responsibility waste + 670 material waste, with a material waste rate of 42.6%. Rust (516 pcs) and stain (124 pcs) are dominant causes — anti-rust and storage management need improvement.
✅ 前磨控制良好✅ Front Grinding Well Controlled
前磨总废品仅 265件，尺寸超差（Size）是主因占 91%，两道工序（端面/无心）整体稳定可控。 Front-grinding total waste is only 265 pcs. Dimensional out-of-spec (Size) is the primary cause at 91%. Both processes (End Face / Centerless) are stable and well-controlled.
🔍 生锈是料废第一因🔍 Rust — #1 Material Waste Cause
后磨料废中生锈（Rust）达 1,197件，占 68.3%，在OR、ORSF、IRSF三道工序均有大量出现，防锈管控需系统性改善。 Rust accounts for 1,197 pcs of back-grinding material waste — 68.3% of the total — appearing across OR, ORSF, and IRSF processes. Anti-rust control requires a systematic overhaul.

生锈（Rust）缺陷 3,676件，占所有外观缺陷总量的 28.5%，在6个型号中均排名前三，系统性防锈管理需全面改善。 Rust defects account for 3,676 pcs — 28.5% of all appearance defects — ranking in the top 3 across all 6 models. Systematic anti-rust management needs a comprehensive overhaul.
